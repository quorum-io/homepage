How It Works

Democratic decisions on a network you control

Real Group Consensus

Proposals only pass when 67% of the community agrees. Every voice is weighted by trust earned through participation—and no one can vote on their own proposals.

Votes decay over 5 minutes • One vote per person

Tamper-Proof Records

Every piece of content gets a unique fingerprint. If anyone changes even a single character, the fingerprint won't match. Duplicates are automatically detected.

SHA-256 content addressing

Quantum-Resistant Security

Built with Dilithium3 signatures—cryptography designed to withstand attacks from quantum computers. Your data stays secure not just today, but decades from now.

Post-quantum lattice-based cryptography

Private Conversations

Send direct messages that only you and the recipient can read. Messages are encrypted end-to-end using modern cryptography, and both parties must consent to start a conversation.

X25519 key exchange • ChaCha20Poly1305 encryption

Content That Stays Relevant

Unused content naturally fades after 7 days—but the community can vote to pin important information permanently or prune what's no longer needed.

Engagement-based decay • Democratic pinning

Find Communities Easily

Discover pools through a public directory—search by topic to find communities that match your interests. Register your own pool so others can find you.

DHT-based discovery • Topic registration

Principles

Built on fairness, security, and transparency

01

People Verify People

New members explain why they want to join, and existing members decide whether to let them in. This keeps bots and bad actors out without requiring expensive proof-of-work.

02

Earn Your Influence

You can't vote on your own proposals. Your voting power grows as you participate constructively—quality contributions matter more than just showing up.

03

Layered Protection

Multiple security measures work together: rate limiting stops spam, signatures verify identity, and size limits prevent abuse. Each layer assumes the others might fail.

04

Easy to Explore

Anyone can browse the network and discover communities without signing up first. Find what you're looking for, then decide if you want to join.

05

Truth from the Source

The network stores what people actually said—everything else is calculated from that record. No hidden edits, no backdoor changes, just verifiable history.

06

Ready for the Future

Today's encryption can be broken by tomorrow's quantum computers. We use quantum-resistant cryptography from day one—no stressful migration needed later.

Network

Communities that connect on their own terms

Quorum

Pools Are Communities

Each pool is a private space for a group. Members share a passphrase to join—like a members-only club where everyone has the key. Different organizations can use the same network without seeing each other's data.

Share What You Want

Send images, videos, and documents directly to your community. Large files are split into chunks that reassemble automatically, with built-in verification that nothing was corrupted along the way.

Threads Keep Context

Reply directly to any message to create threaded discussions. See the full conversation tree, nested as deep as it needs to go. Never lose track of who said what to whom.
