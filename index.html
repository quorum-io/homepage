Principles

The foundations that guide our engineering

01

Human in the Loop

Every connection requires human elaboration and approval. Sybil resistance through social proof, not capital.

02

Skin in the Game

Self-voting is prohibited. Proposers cannot vote on their own proposals. Trust is earned through quality.

03

Defense in Depth

Rate limiting, replay protection, signature verification. Every layer assumes the others have failed.

04

Open Discovery

Anyone can probe the network to find pools and peers. Bootstrapping requires only a single known address.

05

Derived State

Store expressions, compute everything else. The log is the source of truth. State is a view.

06

Quantum Ready

Post-quantum signatures from day one. We build for the threats that don't exist yet.
